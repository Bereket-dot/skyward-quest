SKYWARD QUEST

Embark on an epic journey through floating islands. Collect gems, defeat enemies, and unlock ancient secrets!

SETTINGS

HOW TO PLAY

Objective

Navigate through floating islands, collect gems, defeat enemies, and reach the end of each level.

Controls

W or ↑ - Jump

A or ← - Move Left

D or → - Move Right

SPACE - Attack

P - Pause Game

M - Toggle Mute

Game Elements

Gems - Collect for points

Health - Restore health

Locked Door - Requires key to open

Key - Unlocks doors

Enemies - Avoid or defeat
